VOC / Sewer Sensor Guide

The most common sources of VOC's in the home are actively growing mold and Sewer Gas.

Actively Growing Mold
To grow, mold requires a food source such as leaves, paper, wood or dirt.
It also requires moisture.

When actively growing, the mold will emit spores into the air. By cleaning with an appropriate vacuum ( see Vacuum Cleaner Selection ). A thorough cleaning can reduce the smell in the house and reduce the mold spores / dust & mycotoxins and may help the occupants feel better. Cleaning may help to find the source of the smell (with your nose) but it is unlikely to have any effect on the VOC / Sewer gas readings on the sensor.
