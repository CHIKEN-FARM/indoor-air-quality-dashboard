Particulate Matter

Sources in the home
In a properly maintained home, most of the airborne particulate matter comes from the outside. However, some homes do have significant sources of indoor particulate matter which come from the following sources:
  • Cigarette Smoking
  • Cooking
  • Malfunctioning combustion appliances
  • Non-vented combustion appliances
  • Wood-burning appliances
  • Mould Growth

Health
Increase in the following symptoms (especially in children and pets) :
  • Coughing
  • Wheezing
